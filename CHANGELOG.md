### v1.5.0 — 2026-04-28

#### Manuscript corrections
- **Optimal phase band renamed** from x.18°E to x.21°E throughout — the band
  label is now driven by the `\xbandLabel` macro (emitted by
  `analysis/unesco/verify_x18_periodicity.py`) so it tracks the A+ threshold
  automatically; note added that the exact phase is tier-definition-dependent.
- **Corpus mismatch fixed** in `analysis/global/x18_max_permutation_test.py`:
  observed max now computed on the same N = 1011 corpus used for the null
  (extended corpus minus Gerizim synthetic); corrected permutation p-value
  updated in macros (`anchorMaxPermP`, `anchorMaxPermZ`).
- **Cluster radius macro** `\clusterRadiusDeg` added (= TIER_A_MAX × BERU =
  0.19°); replaces three hardcoded `0.3°` values in manuscript.
- **Self-exclusion wording** in anchor sweep description simplified from
  "within 0.001°" to "each anchor excluding its own entry"; `\NgerizimSweepCorpus`
  corrected from 1010 to 1011 and now computed from the extended corpus in
  `analysis/global/anchor_site_comparison.py`.
- **Conclusion** redrafted to add temporal gradient, anchor specificity, and
  OWTRAD as third independent line of evidence.

#### Religion sub-population analysis
- **Hinduism sentence** rewritten to match Buddhism's (A-tier count; C-band
  count; joint p) format; macro `\NhinATier` added to
  `analysis/unesco/origin_sites_test.py`.
- **Exclusive-subset check** added: both Buddhism (p = 0.013) and Hinduism
  (p = 0.003) joint A/C signals persist after removing the 14 co-tagged sites,
  confirming the pattern is independent. New macros: `\pBudExclJoint`,
  `\pHinExclJoint`, `\NBudHinShared`.

#### New audit file
- **`supplementary/audit/owtrad_audit.txt`** — full reproducibility audit for
  the OWTRAD Silk Road harmonic alignment analysis: binomial results for all
  three populations and all three tiers, phase-shift permutation (100,000
  iterations), node-label permutation (100,000 iterations), cluster asymmetry
  test, and complete A++/A+ vertex listing with coordinates and degree.
  Generated by `tools/generate_audit_owtrad.py`.
